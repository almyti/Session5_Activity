What is ZARA?

ZARA is a Spanish fashion retailer founded in 1975. It is one of the largest international fashion companies, operating over 2,200 stores in 96 countries. ZARA specializes in fast fashion, offering trendy clothes, shoes and accessories at competitive prices. Its collections are updated twice a week, allowing customers to stay up-to-date with the latest trends.
